# Weekly Session Review

Let's review what's been captured and surface valuable patterns.

## What I'll Do

1. **Read** `.claude/sessions/captures.md`
2. **Identify** recurring themes or solutions
3. **Suggest** patterns worth promoting
4. **Clean up** outdated or one-off captures

## Questions I'll Answer

- What solutions have been used multiple times?
- Any gotchas that should be documented?
- Anything worth sharing with the broader team?

## Output

I'll provide:
- Summary of captured insights
- Recommended patterns to formalize
- Suggestions for cleanup

## Time Required

5 minutes, once per week. The flywheel compounds.
